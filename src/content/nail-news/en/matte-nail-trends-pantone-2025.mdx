## Matte Nails: The Quiet Luxury of No Shine

Beyond the world of glossy and chrome, matte nail finishes stand out through texture alone. In spring/summer 2025, this "anti-shine" movement pairs beautifully with Pantone's seasonal color palette.

## What Is a Matte Nail?

Matte polish or gel contains no shine-enhancing ingredients, resulting in a velvet-like surface. The visual effect is calmer and more tactile than traditional high-gloss nails.

## Pantone 2025 Spring/Summer Colors × Matte Finish

### Peachy Apricot (Peach Fuzz Derivative)
A warm peach-orange in matte form feels more mature and elegant than its glossy counterpart — great for everyday wear.

### Lavender Purple
A soft, cool-toned lilac rendered matte looks like a dried flower — romantic without being fussy.

### Olive Green
An earthy olive, championed by fashion bloggers in 2025, reads as quietly intellectual in a matte finish.

### Creamy White
The perennially wearable creamy white gains a layer of refinement in matte — a true go-anywhere shade.

### Oat Brown
A latte-warm brown in matte looks remarkably natural and flatters a wide range of skin tones.

## Matte Nail Care Tips

1. **Avoid oils**: Cuticle oil and hand cream can restore shine to matte areas — apply carefully.
2. **Use a matte top coat**: Reapply periodically to maintain the flat finish.
3. **Clean with alcohol wipes**: A light swipe removes surface oils and extends the matte effect.
4. **Prevent scratching**: Matte surfaces show scratches more readily than glossy ones.

## Matte vs. Glossy: How to Choose

| Trait | Matte | Glossy |
|-------|-------|--------|
| Visual Effect | Understated, calm | Eye-catching, vibrant |
| Durability | Moderate | Higher |
| Scratch Visibility | More noticeable | Less noticeable |
| Best For | Office, artistic settings | Daily wear, parties |
