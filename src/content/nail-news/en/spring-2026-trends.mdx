## Spring 2026 Nail Trends

Spring is here, and it's time for a fresh nail look! Here are the standout trends for this season.

## Top 5 Styles

### 1. Cream Tones
Soft cream whites, nude pinks, and light apricot shades for understated elegance.

### 2. Chrome & Metallic
Silver and gold mirror effects that bring a futuristic edge.

### 3. Minimalist Lines
Fine lines, dots, and geometric patterns — less is more.

### 4. Garden Impressions
Hand-painted florals and vines full of spring vibes.

### 5. Jelly Transparency
Translucent jelly textures in soft colors — fresh and playful.
