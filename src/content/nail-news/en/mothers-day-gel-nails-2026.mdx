## 2026 Mother's Day Gel Nail Trends

### 1. Advanced Glazed Nails (Upgraded)

The glazed donut nails trend evolved this year — add chrome powder for a glass-water effect. Universally flattering, works on all skin tones, and looks elegant without being loud. **Best for: moms who love subtle sophistication.**

### 2. Jelly Nails (Soft & Sweet)

Semi-transparent candy colors — peach, grape purple, sky blue — give nails a dewy, youthful look. **Best for: trendy moms or those who love a pop of color.**

### 3. Matte Chrome

Matte base + metallic shimmer overlay. Rose gold and champagne gold are this year's top picks. **Best for: fashion-forward moms who want statement nails.**

### 4. Classic French (Timeless)

Never goes out of style. 2026's French tips feature a subtle curved edge instead of the straight line — more modern, more elongating. **Best for: professional moms or those who prefer understated elegance.**

## Aftercare Tips to Share with Mom

1. **Apply cuticle oil 2x daily** — morning and night — to keep the gel edge flexible and prevent lifting
2. **Never use nails as tools** — opening cans, peeling stickers are the #1 cause of gel chipping
3. **Wear gloves when washing dishes** — prolonged hot water exposure accelerates gel oxidation
4. **Return for a refill in 3–4 weeks** — before the gel grows out too far
5. **Never peel gel off** — always soak off properly with foil wraps to protect natural nails
