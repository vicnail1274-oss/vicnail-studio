2026 spring/summer is bringing a color revolution to the nail world. After years of quiet, understated neutrals dominating the scene, this season's gel nail palette is surging back with energy — think soft, saturated "pastel-cream" hues that are vivid without being loud.

---

## The Season's Color Story: The Soft-Color Era

Unlike the nude-muted dominance of 2024–2025, this season opens up the color spectrum while keeping that signature low-saturation softness. Lavender, butter yellow, mint, coral, and berry rose are all sharing the spotlight — unified by one quality: **milky-toned, wearable, and forgiving across skin tones.**

---

## 7 Trending Gel Nail Shades for Spring/Summer 2026

### 1. Butter Yellow

Butter yellow is the undisputed breakout shade of 2026 spring/summer. It's not fluorescent — think warm, cream-white-based yellow that looks like a tiny square of sunshine on your fingertip.

**Best for:** Lighter skin tones for maximum vibrancy; opt for an ivory-leaning version on warm/yellow undertones to balance.
**Wear it:** As a solid single coat, or pair with a fine white French tip for fresh, clean elegance.

---

### 2. Lavender Mist

Lavender has finally graduated from niche to mainstream in 2026. The matte-finish version is especially sought after, projecting an understated, cool sophistication.

**Best for:** Fair and pink-cool undertones; add shimmer topcoat if you have olive or warm skin to brighten it up.
**Wear it:** Matte topcoat over a clean solid lavender — the most-requested salon finish this season.

---

### 3. Mint Green

Mint green carries a natural cooling effect — the most "relaxing" nail color of summer. The 2026 version skews "white-green," dialing back the intensity while keeping that refreshing freshness.

**Best for:** Works across skin tones; warmer complexions should choose a gray-mint to avoid yellow-green clashing.
**Wear it:** Mint green with a white double French tip, or as a simple solid on short nails.

---

### 4. Coral Orange

Coral orange is summer's perennial bestseller, and 2026's version trends warmer — blending orange-red energy with soft pink warmth. It reads polished without trying too hard.

**Best for:** Particularly flattering on warm, yellow, and olive skin — has a brightening effect.
**Wear it:** Solid coral with a gold thin-line French border for double the refinement.

---

### 5. Berry Rose

Berry rose is the season's "ambition shade" — deeper than pink, softer than red, radiating a quietly confident maturity. For those who want spring/summer nails with a hint of mystery.

**Best for:** Fairest skin tones for maximum impact; also stunning on deeper complexions for a luxurious effect.
**Wear it:** Matte berry rose with a reverse French design — clear base, berry smile line.

---

### 6. Sky Blue

Sky blue arrives in 2026 with two compelling variations: "haze blue" (grayer, moodier) and "baby blue" (lighter, milkier). Both have become summer shorthand for coastal, easy-going style.

**Best for:** Baby blue for lighter skin tones; haze blue is more versatile and flattering on warm undertones.
**Wear it:** Baby blue solid, or add a white 3D cloud accent for a playful seasonal touch.

---

### 7. Peach Apricot

Peach apricot is the "safest bet" in the lineup — stylish but timeless, easy to wear, and genuinely long-lasting as a trend. This year's version leans toward a transparent peach with a glass-like semi-sheer quality.

**Best for:** Universally flattering — the top recommendation for gift vouchers and first-timer bookings.
**Wear it:** Translucent peach apricot with a holographic shimmer topcoat for a "nail crystal" effect.

---

## How to Choose from 7 Shades

| Occasion | Best Pick |
|---|---|
| Daily commute / office | Peach apricot, butter yellow |
| Dates / photography | Berry rose, lavender mist |
| Summer travel | Sky blue, mint green |
| Formal events | Coral orange (gold French) |

---

The spirit of 2026 spring/summer colorful gel nails is simple: **have color, but let it complement you — not compete with you.** The right shade makes every outfit feel considered, and your nails become the smallest, most personal statement of your day.
