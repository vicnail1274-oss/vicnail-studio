Spring/summer 2026 nail trends can be summed up in one phrase: "Nails that look freshly moisturized are the most beautiful nails." Leaving behind last year's heavy glitter era, the three dominant directions this season — jelly, glass, and cream — all share a common aesthetic of **translucency, softness, and refinement**. Whether you're a nail beginner or a seasoned enthusiast, these three styles deserve a place on your nail calendar.

## Jelly Nails: Candy-Transparent Color

### What Are Jelly Nails?

Inspired by translucent soft candy, jelly nails feature semi-transparent color — tinted but still able to show the natural nail beneath. The result looks like a crystallized sugar candy has been placed on your fingertip.

### The Hottest Jelly Colors in 2026

- **Jelly Pink**: Rose-toned with high transparency, perfect for spring dates and afternoon gatherings
- **Jelly Orange**: Warm, translucent amber tones bursting with summer energy
- **Jelly Lavender**: Soft purple transparency — gentle yet with character
- **Jelly Cola**: Deep, translucent brown that surprisingly works year-round

### How to Apply Jelly Nails

1. Start with a clear base coat
2. Choose a semi-transparent gel polish (look for "Jelly" or "Sheer" in the product name)
3. Apply 2–3 thin layers, curing each layer under LED/UV
4. Finish with a glossy top coat to amplify the candy-like clarity

**Best for:** Daily wear, dates, casual outings
**Difficulty:** ★★☆☆☆

## Glass Nails: Mirror-Reflective Fingertips

### What Are Glass Nails?

Glass nails chase the ultimate reflectivity — the nail surface is smooth as a mirror, catching light and creating brilliant high-shine points that resemble light refracting through glass. This effect is typically achieved through:

- **Mirror powder technique**: Apply silver-white mirror powder with a sponge applicator
- **Chrome powder technique**: Rich in color options — rose gold, ice blue, rainbow aurora
- **Ultra-gloss top coat only**: No powder needed, just a high-shine top coat for clean luminosity

### Glass Nail Color Trends in 2026

| Shade | Effect | Best for |
|-------|--------|----------|
| Silver mirror | Cool, sharp | Formal events, parties |
| Rose gold | Warm, luxurious | Dates, weddings |
| Rainbow aurora | Dreamy, iridescent | Special occasions |
| Ice blue transparent | Fresh, futuristic | Summer casual |

### Application Tips

The most critical step for glass nails is a **perfectly smooth nail surface**. Any scratches or air bubbles become dramatically visible under high gloss, so:

1. Thoroughly push back cuticles and buff the nail surface before applying base coat
2. Apply gel polish evenly, avoiding excess buildup at the edges
3. If air bubbles appear before top coat, pop them with a nail art pin before curing

**Best for:** Parties, photos, special events, celebrations
**Difficulty:** ★★★☆☆

## Cream Nails: Soft, Velvety Elegance

### What Are Cream Nails?

Cream nails aren't a single color — they're a family of "white-added" soft tones. From cream white and cream nude to cream blush, cream butter, and cream latte, they all belong to the cream color family. What they share: understated but polished, gentle but high-quality, versatile enough to match anything in your wardrobe.

### 2026 Cream Color Recommendations

- **Cream White**: The purest cream — flattering on any nail length
- **Cream Nude**: Closest to skin tone for maximum natural effect, ideal for the workplace
- **Cream Blush**: Pink meets cream — soft and feminine
- **Cream Butter**: The breakout new color of 2026, low saturation but surprisingly striking
- **Cream Latte**: A deeper cream brown that works year-round

### Application Tips for Cream Nails

The soul of cream nails lies in **saturated, even coverage**:

1. Choose cream-consistency gel polish (thicker texture, lower flow)
2. One smooth stroke per section — don't re-brush the same area (causes streaks)
3. Apply 2 coats: first coat thin, second coat full coverage
4. Finish with a semi-matte or satin top coat for a "velvet cream" luxury feel

**Best for:** Work, daily wear, wedding guest, travel
**Difficulty:** ★☆☆☆☆
