## Why Does a Nail Artist Need AI?

As a nail studio owner, daily tasks include scheduling, replying to clients, posting on social media, bookkeeping... These repetitive tasks eat up so much time.

AI isn't here to replace the nail artist's craft — it's here to handle the mechanical work so we can focus on creating.

## My Automation Toolkit

### Scheduling System
Built a custom scheduling app with React where students can check availability and book sessions.

### Auto-Replies
LINE official account paired with AI to automatically answer common questions, reducing 80% of repetitive inquiries.

### Social Media Automation
AI helps generate post content and schedule publishing, making social media management stress-free.

## For Those Who Want to Try

You don't need to be an engineer to start. Begin with the simplest tools and build up from there.
