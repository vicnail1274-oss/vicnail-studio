## Why Your Nail Salon Needs a LINE Bot

If you're a nail technician in Taiwan, this scenario is painfully familiar:

- You're mid-gel application, and your phone won't stop buzzing
- You answer the same questions every day: "Are you available Saturday?" "How much is a gel set?" "Do I need to book in advance?"
- You want to rest after hours, but you're afraid of missing potential bookings

A LINE Bot solves all three problems: it handles routine inquiries automatically, accepts booking interest 24 hours a day, and lets you focus on your clients without constant interruptions.

This guide walks you through setup from scratch — no engineers, no coding required.

---

## Foundation: Setting Up LINE Official Account

### Step 1: Create Your LINE Official Account

1. Visit [LINE for Business](https://www.linebiz.com/tw/) and apply for a LINE Official Account (LINE OA)
2. Select "General Account" (free tier) to start
3. Fill in your business name, category (Beauty / Nail), and contact details

The free plan includes **200 broadcast messages per month** with **unlimited auto-replies** — more than sufficient for most independent nail studios.

### Step 2: Configure Basic Auto-Replies

In LINE OA Manager:
1. Navigate to "Auto-Reply Messages"
2. Add keyword-triggered replies for your most common questions:

| Keyword(s) | Auto-Reply Content |
|------------|--------------------|
| price, cost, how much | "Gel nail sets start from $800 for solid colors, $1,200+ for nail art designs. View our full price list here: [link]" |
| booking, available, appointment | "We'd love to book you in! Please let us know your preferred date and time, and we'll confirm shortly. You can also book online: [link]" |
| address, location, directions | "We're located at [address], 5 minutes from [MRT station] Exit [X]. Google Maps: [link]" |
| hours, open, schedule | "We're open Tuesday–Sunday, 10:00 AM–8:00 PM. Closed Mondays." |

These keywords cover roughly **80% of typical inquiries** and activate immediately after setup.

## Advanced: Making Your Bot Sound Human

### Use ChatGPT to Write Natural-Sounding Replies

LINE OA's default reply templates tend to feel robotic. Ask ChatGPT to rewrite them with warmth:

> **"I'm an independent nail artist in Taiwan. Please rewrite the following customer service reply to sound warmer and more natural: 'Our gel nail pricing is as follows…'"**

When your bot communicates in a friendly, human tone, clients feel better about the interaction — even knowing it's automated.

### Set Up a Rich Menu

A Rich Menu is an interactive button panel displayed at the bottom of the chat window. Design it to give clients instant access to key information:

```
┌─────────────────────────────┐
│  📋 View Prices  │  📅 Book Now  │
│─────────────────┼────────────────│
│  📍 Our Location │  💬 Contact Us │
└─────────────────────────────┘
```

To set it up: LINE OA Manager → Rich Menu → Create New

Clients arriving in your chat can find what they need in one tap — no waiting, no back-and-forth.

### Integrate a Third-Party AI Chatbot (Advanced)

For smarter conversations — like AI that can respond to "I want something spring-inspired for a date, what do you recommend?" — consider:

- **ManyChat**: Integrates LINE and Instagram with basic AI conversation flows
- **Landbot**: Drag-and-drop conversation builder, great for complex booking flows
- **n8n + ChatGPT API**: Higher technical barrier but fully customizable; enables genuine AI-powered conversations

## Booking Integration: Let Your Bot Collect Appointments

The ideal setup:
1. Client messages asking about availability
2. LINE Bot auto-replies: "What date are you looking to book?"
3. After receiving a response, bot sends a Google Form or Calendly link
4. Client fills out the form; automated confirmation message is sent

You can build this entire flow using **Google Forms + LINE OA auto-replies** in under 30 minutes.

## FAQ

**Q: Does LINE Bot cost money?**
LINE OA's core features — including unlimited auto-replies — are free. Advanced features (like API integration for custom chatbots) require a Messaging API account, which is free to apply for but requires some technical knowledge.

**Q: Will clients find it weird talking to a bot?**
As long as your reply copy sounds natural, most clients won't mind. The key insight: getting a fast, accurate answer from a bot beats waiting 20 minutes for a manual reply.

**Q: Do I need a developer?**
Not for basic keyword auto-replies. Rich Menu design requires basic image sizing knowledge. Advanced AI integrations will need technical help.
