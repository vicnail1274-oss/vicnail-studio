## What Is Midjourney? What Do You Need?

Midjourney is an AI image generation tool that creates high-quality visuals from text descriptions. You type a prompt, and within seconds it generates four different variations for you to choose from.

**What you need:**
- A Discord account (free)
- A Midjourney subscription (starting around $10/month)
- Writing prompts in English (English prompts produce significantly better results)

While there's a cost, for nail professionals the value is clear: unlimited AI-generated nail art reference images for the price of a coffee per week.

## The Core Skill: Writing Prompts That Generate Beautiful Nail Art

Prompt quality directly determines output quality. Here's an efficient structure formula:

```
[nail style] nail art, [color description], [finish/texture],
[detail description], close-up macro photography, high detail,
white background
```

### Prompt Comparison: Bad vs. Good

**Weak prompt:**
```
beautiful nail art
```
→ Generic output with no distinct style

**Strong prompt:**
```
minimalist nail art, soft beige and dusty rose, matte finish,
thin gold line details, almond shape, close-up macro photography,
high detail, white background
```
→ Specific, stylish, and usable as a client reference image

## Advanced Tips: Getting Results Closer to Real Nails

### Tip 1: Specify Nail Shape

Midjourney is sensitive to nail shape keywords. Adding these makes the output significantly more accurate:
- `almond shape`
- `coffin shape`
- `oval shape`
- `square shape`
- `stiletto shape`

### Tip 2: Define Finish and Texture

- `matte finish`
- `glossy gel finish`
- `chrome mirror effect`
- `cat eye effect`
- `glitter texture`

### Tip 3: Add Photography Parameters for Realism

Append to any prompt:
```
close-up macro photography, 85mm lens, professional studio lighting,
high resolution, photorealistic
```

This pushes the output toward photorealistic nail photography rather than illustration style.

### Tip 4: Set Aspect Ratio with `--ar`

For nail art content, `--ar 1:1` or `--ar 4:3` works great for Instagram and Pinterest:
```
[your prompt] --ar 1:1
```

## How Nail Professionals Can Use AI-Generated Images at Work

### Use Case 1: Build a Style Portfolio Before You Have Real Work

New nail artists can use Midjourney to generate multiple style directions and build a visual style board — giving potential clients a sense of what aesthetics you work in.

**Important**: Label these clearly as "AI-generated inspiration" and don't present them as photos of your actual work.

### Use Case 2: Visual Confirmation Before the Appointment

When a client describes their desired design at booking, generate a close approximation for them to confirm before they arrive. This dramatically reduces "it doesn't look like what I imagined" moments.

### Use Case 3: Seasonal Trend Inspiration Library

Before each season, generate 20–30 images in trending styles and post them to your Instagram or LINE account as a voting poll. Let clients pick what they want. Free market research.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Can I use Midjourney-generated images commercially?**
A: Paid subscribers generally have commercial usage rights, but check Midjourney's current Terms of Service for the latest rules.

**Q: Can I write prompts in Chinese?**
A: Midjourney understands Chinese, but English prompts consistently produce better results. If you think in Chinese, use ChatGPT to translate your description into an English prompt first.

**Q: The output doesn't match what I want. What do I do?**
A: Try adding `--no [unwanted element]` to exclude things you don't want, and make your color/detail descriptions more specific.
