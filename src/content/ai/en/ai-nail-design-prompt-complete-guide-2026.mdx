## Prompt Structure Basics: The Language of Nail Design

Across all tools, an effective nail design prompt typically follows this structure:

```
[style] + [color] + [texture/effect] + [decorative details] + [mood/aesthetic] + [technical specs]
```

### Example Breakdown

**Goal:** Magnolia pink pearl-shimmer French nails with delicate white florals

**Assembled Prompt:**
```
nail art design, magnolia pink gel nails,
French tip style with soft white floral line art,
pearl shimmer effect, glossy finish,
elegant and feminine aesthetic,
close-up macro photography, studio lighting,
detailed nail art reference photo
```

## Advanced Tips: Keywords That Make AI Nail Images More Realistic

**Lighting and photography:**
- `natural lighting` — most realistic
- `salon lighting` — shows gloss beautifully
- `macro close-up` — captures fine detail
- `studio photography` — clean, editorial look

**Texture and material:**
- `gel nail texture` — explicitly specifies gel (not acrylic or polish)
- `glossy finish` / `matte finish` — surface specification
- `real nail photography` — emphasizes photorealism
- `highly detailed` — pushes for precision

**Negative prompts (for Midjourney and SD):**
```
--no cartoon, illustration, unrealistic, fake, plastic, artificial
```

## From AI Image to Salon: Communicating Effectively With Your Nail Tech

### Step 1: Generate Multiple Angles

Use variations of the same prompt to generate front, side, and top-down views. Your nail tech needs to see edge wrapping and profile shape, not just the face-on view.

**Midjourney tip:** Add `from above`, `side view`, or `front view` to control perspective.

### Step 2: Annotate What You Love

Screenshot the AI image and mark: "I specifically love this color," "keep this floral pattern," "I don't need this rhinestone."

### Step 3: Communicate Flexibility

Tell your nail tech: "This is an AI inspiration image. The color can be matched to what you have available, but I want to keep this overall style."

### Step 4: Discuss Technical Feasibility

Some AI details require high skill levels (ultra-fine linework, complex 3D structures). Ask upfront about pricing and whether your tech specializes in that style.

## Common Problems and Solutions

**Problem 1: The AI-generated hand looks distorted**

Fix: Add `flat lay nail display, not showing hands` or `professional nail art reference photo, close-up only`. This avoids generating full hands and focuses on the nails themselves.

---

**Problem 2: Colors are inaccurate**

Fix: Use precise color descriptions:
- ❌ `pink nails`
- ✅ `dusty rose pink, hex #e8b4b8, desaturated warm pink, muted not bright`

---

**Problem 3: Results look like illustration, not real nails**

Fix: Reinforce photorealism keywords:
```
photorealistic, real nail polish texture,
nail art reference photo,
not illustration, not artwork, not cartoon
```

---

**Problem 4: Results vary too much — can't control style consistency**

Fix (Midjourney): Use `--seed [number]` to lock the random seed, keeping a consistent base aesthetic across generations. Then use `--vary (subtle)` for controlled adjustments.
