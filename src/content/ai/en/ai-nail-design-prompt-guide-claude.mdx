## Generate Dream Nail Designs with Claude AI: Complete Prompt Guide

Clients sit down and say: "I have no idea what I want — can you decide for me?"

Before AI, this relied entirely on a nail technician's experience and imagination. Now, Claude AI is your creative partner. This guide shows you how to use the right prompts to get 5–10 specific, actionable design concepts in 30 seconds.

---

## Why Claude?

There are many AI tools out there. Here's how they compare for nail design work:

| Tool | Strength | Best for nail work |
|------|----------|--------------------|
| **Claude** | Strong text comprehension, detailed descriptions, conversational | Design brainstorming, client communication, descriptions |
| **Midjourney** | Generates actual images | Visual mockups, color palette boards |
| **ChatGPT** | General purpose | Marketing copy, social media posts |

**The short version:** Claude is excellent at *describing designs in words*. Midjourney is excellent at *drawing them*. The power combo: use Claude to develop the concept, then feed the description into Midjourney to generate the visual.

## 5 Categories of Ready-to-Use Prompt Templates

### 1. Seasonal Color Palette (Most Used)

```
I need 5 gel nail design ideas suitable for [season] in Taiwan.
Requirements:
- Color family: [fresh & light / earth tones / bold & vibrant]
- Style: [minimalist / cute / sophisticated]
- Special needs: [office-appropriate / date night / Japanese-inspired]

For each design, please provide: name, main + accent colors,
suggested nail art elements, and ideal client type.
```

**Sample output (spring season):** Claude might respond with designs like "Matcha Latte" (base: cream + sage green, accent: tiny flower stickers) or "Misty Lavender Gradient" (base: lavender + white, detail: halo moon effect).

---

### 2. Event & Occasion

```
A client is attending [event/occasion]. Suggest 3 gel nail designs.
Event: [wedding guest / Japan trip / company dinner / concert]
Client preferences: [likes/dislikes]
Budget tier: [basic / mid-range / premium]

For each design include: style name, color combo, nail art details,
estimated application time.
```

---

### 3. Client Style Analysis (Communication Helper)

```
Help me figure out what nail style suits this client:
Age range: [18-25 / 25-35 / 35+]
Occupation: [student / office worker / service industry / freelancer]
Typical outfit colors: [neutrals / bold / black & white]
Personality: [outgoing / understated / fashion-forward]

Recommend 3 style directions, with 2 specific designs per direction.
```

This prompt shines when clients say "just surprise me" — helps you give a polished, professional recommendation quickly.

---

### 4. Design Upgrade (Start from Existing)

```
My current design is: [describe the base, e.g., solid beige with one accent nail]
Client wants it to feel "more special, but not too loud."
Suggest 3 upgrade directions:
1. What embellishments to add
2. Color combinations that work
3. Estimated price increase compared to the base design (use percentages)
```

---

### 5. Instagram Caption (Marketing Essential)

```
Write an Instagram caption for this gel nail design:
Design name: [name]
Key features: [2-3 highlights]
Target client: [who it suits]

Requirements:
- 5 relevant hashtags
- Booking CTA at the end
- Tone: [playful / luxe / warm & friendly]
- Length: 80-120 words
```

## Pro Tip: Keep the Conversation Going

Claude's biggest advantage over other AI tools is **conversational refinement**. Not satisfied with the first answer? Just follow up:

- "Make design #3 use more autumn-appropriate colors"
- "Rewrite this in a more casual tone for Instagram"
- "Adapt this for clients with shorter nails"

Every follow-up gets you closer to exactly what you need.

## Practical Workflow (5 Minutes to First Design)

1. Client shares their needs (1 min)
2. Paste requirements into your Claude prompt template (30 sec)
3. Review Claude's response, select 2–3 directions for client to choose from (1 min)
4. Feed the confirmed design description to Midjourney for a reference image (1–2 min)
5. Client approves — start the work

From "I don't know what I want" to confirmed design in under 5 minutes.
