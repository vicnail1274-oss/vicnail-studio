## How Much Time Are You Spending on the Same LINE Replies?

"Do you do gel nails?"
"Is Saturday afternoon available?"
"About how much for a full set?"
"Do gel nail extensions need to be soaked off?"

You probably answer these four questions dozens of times every week.

Each manual reply costs time and energy — and if you don't respond immediately, the client might message another salon instead.

**A LINE Bot combined with AI customer service can automatically handle 80% of routine inquiries, leaving you to deal only with situations that genuinely require your judgment.**

---

## What Can a LINE Bot Do for a Nail Salon?

A LINE Bot is an automation layer on top of your LINE Official Account. With proper setup, it handles:

### 1. Automatic FAQ Replies

A client sends "how much for gel nails" — the Bot immediately returns your complete service menu. No manual copy-paste required, and no missed replies because you were working on a client.

### 2. Booking Link Guidance

A client asks "how do I book?" — the Bot automatically sends the booking link or menu, guiding the client through the online booking flow without your involvement.

### 3. Rich Menu (Bottom Navigation)

LINE Official Account's Rich Menu is the fixed panel at the bottom of the chat. Set up buttons for "Book Now," "Services," "Portfolio," and "Contact Us" — clients know exactly where to find information from their first interaction.

### 4. Return Visit Reminders and Promotions

Gel nails typically need a fill or new set every 3–4 weeks. Set the Bot to automatically send a message 21 days after the last service: "Your gel nails are 3 weeks old — would you like to book a maintenance appointment or try a new style?"

### 5. Post-Service Follow-up

Two days after the appointment, automatically send: "How are you enjoying your gel nails? We'd love to hear your feedback." This creates a personal touch and surfaces any issues early.

## Adding AI Makes the Bot Genuinely Intelligent

A basic LINE Bot works on keyword triggers — the client must type "booking" to trigger the booking flow, or "extension" to receive extension information.

The problem: clients don't speak in your keywords. They might say "I want to get my nails done," "can you fit me in?" or "are you free next Wednesday?" Basic bots can't parse these casual expressions.

**With AI natural language processing**, the Bot understands the intent behind varied phrasing:

- "Are you free next week?" → Automatically guides to booking page
- "Is gel bad for your nails?" → Returns professional explanation
- "What colors are popular right now?" → Recommends current trending styles
- "How do I get them removed?" → Explains gel nail removal process

When clients receive instant, accurate replies, trust in the salon and the owner's professional image both rise significantly.

## Step-by-Step Setup: Building LINE Bot Customer Service from Scratch

**Step 1: Set up a LINE Official Account**

Register at LINE Official Account Manager — the free plan starts at $0 and supports basic Bot features. Upgrade as needed once you exceed 500 messages per month.

**Step 2: Build your FAQ list**

Write down the 20 questions you're asked most often, paired with the standard answers you want the Bot to give. This list is the foundation for all Bot configuration.

Cover at minimum:
- Services and pricing
- How to book
- Hours and address
- Gel nail questions (materials, durability, removal)
- Gel nail extension details

**Step 3: Choose your Bot implementation**

| Approach | Difficulty | Cost | Best For |
|----------|-----------|------|----------|
| LINE Rich Menu + keyword auto-reply | Low | Free | Getting started |
| Third-party Bot platforms (MixerBox, ORCA) | Medium | ~NT$300-800/month | More features without coding |
| Custom Bot + AI integration (NailBot Pro) | Medium-High | Varies | High customization |

**Step 4: Design the Rich Menu**

The Rich Menu is the first thing clients see when they open your account — it directly affects whether they continue engaging. Recommended buttons:
- **Book Now** (links to booking page)
- **Our Services** (illustrated guide with pricing)
- **Portfolio** (links to Instagram or website)
- **Message Owner** (opens direct chat)

**Step 5: Test every flow**

Use your own phone to add your Official Account and test every button and auto-reply. Specifically test edge cases: can the Bot still understand unusual phrasing and provide a reasonable response?

## Real Scenario: How AI Handles a Gel Extension Inquiry

Here's a sample LINE conversation using AI customer service:

> **Client**: Hi, I've never done nail extensions before, wanted to learn more
>
> **Bot (AI)**: Hello! Gel nail extensions use builder gel or nail tips with gel overlay to extend your nails to your desired shape and length. The process takes about 2–3 hours, and they typically last 3–4 weeks. 🌸
>
> Is there anything specific you'd like to know first — cost, materials, maintenance — or would you like to book a quick consultation?

> **Client**: How much does it cost?
>
> **Bot (AI)**: Gel extension pricing depends on length and design — starting from NT$1,200 for a classic style, with additional fees for nail art or special finishes. I can arrange a free 10-minute consultation where the owner can recommend the best option for your nail shape and style preferences. Would that work for you?

This conversation flow requires zero owner involvement but effectively guides the client toward booking.

## Important: Bots Cannot Replace Human Connection

There are situations where the Bot should immediately hand off to you:

1. **Client complaints or dissatisfaction**: Automated replies make complaints worse — you need to respond personally
2. **Complex custom requests**: Wedding nails, bulk orders, or unusual styles need human assessment and custom quotes
3. **Repeated misunderstandings**: If the Bot fails to understand a client three times in a row, it should prompt "Let me connect you with the owner directly"

Set up "transfer to human" triggers to ensure clients can reach you quickly when it truly matters.
