## Post 3 — Gel Nail Myth-Busting 💅

**Headline:** Things clients get wrong about gel nails.

**Caption:**

"Gel ruins your nails."
"It hurts to remove."
"You can't keep it on for more than 2 weeks."

Let's clear this up.

❌ Myth: Gel damages nails.
✅ Truth: Improper *removal* causes damage — not the gel itself. Soak-off technique is everything.

❌ Myth: Gel removal is painful.
✅ Truth: Proper soaking for 15–20 minutes + a wooden stick = zero pain. Pain = someone skipped the soak.

❌ Myth: You must get gel redone every 2 weeks.
✅ Truth: Every 2–3 weeks is ideal. Well-prepped nails with good aftercare can easily stretch to 3 weeks.
