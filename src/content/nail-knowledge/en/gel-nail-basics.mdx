## What Are Gel Nails?

Gel nails use special gel materials that cure under UV or LED light to form a durable, glossy nail coating. Compared to regular nail polish, gel nails last longer and maintain their shine.

## Types of Gel

### 1. Hard Gel
Best for building and extensions. Requires filing to remove.

### 2. Soft Gel (Soak-off Gel)
Can be removed by soaking in acetone. The most common salon gel.

### 3. Builder Gel
Used for nail construction and repair, a middle ground between hard and soft gel.

## Essential Tools

- UV/LED curing lamp
- Base coat
- Color gel
- Top coat
- Nail prep tools
- Cleanser

## Application Tips

1. **Thin layers**: Apply thin coats to prevent wrinkling
2. **Cap the free edge**: Always seal the nail tip
3. **Avoid the cuticle area**: Keep a small gap from the cuticle
4. **Full cure**: Ensure each layer is completely cured under the lamp
