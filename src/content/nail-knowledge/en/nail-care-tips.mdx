## Why Nail Care Matters

Healthy nails are the foundation of great nail art. Even the most stunning designs fall short on unhealthy nails.

## 5 Golden Rules

### 1. Regular Trimming & Shaping
Use a nail file instead of clippers to avoid cracks.

### 2. Keep Moisturized
Apply cuticle oil daily to nourish the nail bed and surrounding skin.

### 3. Avoid Harsh Chemicals
Always wear gloves when using cleaning products.

### 4. Eat Right
Focus on biotin, zinc, and iron-rich foods.

### 5. Give Your Nails a Break
Periodically go gel-free to let your nails recover.
