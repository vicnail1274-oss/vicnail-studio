## Why Do Gel Nails Lift Early?

Gel nails typically last 2–3 weeks, but many people see lifting or peeling within two weeks. The culprit is usually not the technician's skill — it's daily care habits.

## 5 Steps to Extend Your Gel Manicure

### Step 1: Remove All Oil Before the Appointment
Oil is the number one enemy of gel adhesion. If you have naturally oily hands, let your nail tech know so they can do a more thorough prep with alcohol or a dehydrator.

### Step 2: Minimize Contact With Water and Cleaning Products
Wear rubber gloves when washing dishes, doing laundry, or using cleaning agents. Prolonged water exposure seeps under gel edges and accelerates lifting.

### Step 3: Apply Cuticle Oil Daily — But Avoid the Nail Surface
Apply cuticle oil to the skin around your nails (the cuticle area) once a day to keep the skin supple. Do not get oil on the nail plate itself — it will dull the finish.

### Step 4: Stop Using Your Nails as Tools
Opening pull-tabs, scraping off stickers, prying open packaging — these actions put extreme stress on gel nails. Use a tool instead and protect your investment.

### Step 5: Refresh with a Top Coat Every 2–3 Weeks
Return to a salon for a fresh top coat application. This noticeably extends the shine and adhesion, giving your set an extra week of life.

## Common Daily Habits That Damage Gel Nails

- **Heavy typing**: Try using the pads of your fingers instead of your nail tips
- **Frequent hand sanitizer**: Alcohol dries out the gel; apply cuticle oil afterward
- **Long hot showers or baths**: High heat causes gel to expand and contract, weakening adhesion

## When to Go Back for a Fill or Removal

- **Lifting exceeds 1mm**: Return to the salon promptly; attempting to glue it yourself accelerates the risk of infection
- **Nail growth exceeds 3mm**: Book a fill to prevent the nail tip from snapping under leverage
- **Color has significantly darkened or shows cracks**: The gel may be aging — a removal and fresh set is recommended

With the right care routine, getting 4 weeks out of a gel manicure is very achievable.
